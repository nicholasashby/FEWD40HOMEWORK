The List

A new kind of access for ballet fans ages 21–39

Are you age 21-39? Interested in low-price tickets to see one of the world’s finest ballet companies? Want to receive invitations to intimate insider events?

Then get on The List. Here’s how:

  • Register or log in (links below) and provide proof of age—you only have to do this once!
  • Once we've confirmed your proof of age, you’ll be on The List.
  • Every Monday during our season (December to May) we’ll send you an email letting you know if we have $25 tickets that week—act quickly and you could be sitting in premium seats for a fraction of the price!*
  • Then, simply log in to our website and purchase your tickets.

Once you’re on The List, you’ll want to open those weekly emails because they just may include an invitation to an intimate insider event. Watch your email for these exclusive invitations and RSVP quickly—most events will be designed for only a handful of fans.
